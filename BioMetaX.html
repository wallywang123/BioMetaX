Our Novel Biological Technology

We use naturally enriched functional microorganisms to drive metal recovery (Gold, Silver, Nickel, Cobalt, Copper, Vanadium, Chromium, etc.) using waste gases (e.g. methane) from landfills and wastewater treatment plants.

This process reduces greenhouse gas emission while recovering valuable metals from electronic waste, spent batteries, and tailings. We are also developing platforms utilizing carbon dioxide, moving toward zero-carbon solutions.

We welcome collaborations with e-waste recyclers, battery manufacturers, tailings treatment operators, landfill gas suppliers, and wastewater treatment plants worldwide to expand the applications of our technology!
